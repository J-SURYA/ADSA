In terminal(folder_root/):
    mkdir Nqueen
    cd Nqueen
    npm init -y
    npm install express ejs body-parser

Structure of folder Nqueen: 
    Nqueen/
    │
    ├── app.js
    ├── views/
    │   ├── index.ejs
    |   ├── solutions.ejs
    │
    ├── public/
    │   ├── styles.css
    │
    ├── node_modules/
    ├── package.json
    └── package-lock.json



In terminal(folder_root/Nqueen) : 
    node app.js

Your application will be accessible at http://localhost:3000
